2015 Educators of the Year


2015 EDUCATORS OF THE YEAR AWARDS - DINNER BANQUET

Each year, Si Se Puede Foundation honors educators and community advocates within the Chandler Unified School District and Chandler-Gilbert Community College who have displayed outstanding personal and professional characteristics and have made a positive impact on students and the community. More than 450 people attend the Educator Awards Dinner, including many teachers and administrators, community members and government leaders. Our 16th annual awards dinner will be held at the Wild Horse Pass Hotel & Casino, 5040 Wildhorse Pass Boulevard, Chandler, Az. On April 2, 2016.

Sponsorships are now available for this special event. All proceeds from the event, help support SSPF's educational programs. SSPF would like to thank all of our dinner sponsors and give a special thank you to Amica for being this year’s presenting sponsor. This year, SSPF was proud to honor the following individuals:
