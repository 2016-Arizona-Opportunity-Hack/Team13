International Baccalaureate Immersion Camp

Offered by Chandler High School & Si Se Puede Foundation

Dates: Monday, Sept. 29 - Friday, Oct. 3 (9:00am - 12:00pm)
Location: Chandler High School

THIS ONE-WEEK IMMERSION CAMP IS INTENDED FOR 8TH GRADE STUDENTS WITH A DESIRE TO MAKE CONNECTIONS BETWEEN SUBJECTS AND EXPAND THEIR LEARNING.

Goals:

Students will work directly with CHS IB faculty and students to do hands-on activities related to a variety of subject areas. Students will have a deeper understanding of the IB Global Contexts:

  • Scientific and Technical Innovation

  • Identities and Relationships

  • Fairness and Development

  • Dimensions of Time and Space

  • Personal and Cultural Expression

  • Students will have a deeper understanding of how all subject areas are interconnected.

  • Students will reflect on their learning.


The IB Learner Profile will be at the center of all learning. IB Learners strive to be:

  • Open minded

  • Caring

  • Courageous

  • Balanced

  • Reflective

  • Inquirers

  • Knowledgeable

  • Thinkers

  • Communicators

  • Principled
