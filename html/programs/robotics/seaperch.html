ABOUT US Si Se Puede Foundation is a prevention and advocacy organization that encourages its participants to be stakeholders in their community. The organization's three main service components include educational programming, community activities & advocacy, and recreational activities for youth. The organization focuses on school and community risk factors that are barriers to educational achievement including, delinquency, gang affiliation, and family/school/community detachment.
