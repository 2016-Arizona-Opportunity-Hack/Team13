Dance

Now more than ever our young people are in need of creative outlets for communication and self-exploration. The SSPF dance team is committed to providing a free dance program for students of the Chandler Unified School District. As part of the Si Se Puede Foundation, we strive to teach children the importance of teamwork, dedication, respect, and kindness through the art of dance. Our number one priority is to make sure that every child who participates in this program feels part of a safe community where they are able to be themselves.

Our dance program offers many forms of dance and including: baile folklorico, country western, big band and rock n' roll.

Students meet at Hartford Sylvia Encinas Elementary School's Cafeteria, 700 N. Hartford, Chandler on Mondays and Wednesdays from 4:30 PM to 6:30 PM. For more information on how to join/patron/ or request a free performance, please email Ms. Celia Cantabrana at sisepuede.dance@gmail.com

ABOUT US
Si Se Puede Foundation is a prevention and advocacy organization that encourages its participants to be stakeholders in their community. The organization's three main service components include educational programming, community activities & advocacy, and recreational activities for youth. The organization focuses on school and community risk factors that are barriers to educational achievement including, delinquency, gang affiliation, and family/school/community detachment.
